  1. Option 1: The first person to hunt a sperm whale thought that the liquid found inside of the cetacean's head was sperm, due to it's consistency.
  2. Cetaceans can be divided into two groups: Toothed whales (Odontoceti) and Baleen whales (Mysticeti). Toothed whales have teeth, perfect for eating fish, squid, rays, and other marine animals. Baleen whales, on the other hand, have baleen plates that are made out keratin, the same component as human hair and nails, instead of teeth. This helps them feed on small organisms like krill and plankton.
